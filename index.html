Gumba
Italian • Veg-Friendly • New Location on Alberta

Gumba just moved to a new spot on Alberta — the old Bonne Chance location (RIP, more on that below). If you're rolling with vegans or vegetarians, this is the move. Limited options but everything hits. Don't sleep on this one.

2203 NE Alberta St., Portland, OR 97211
